# Season how-to manual

Plain-language notes for things that need doing once a season, or once a gameweek. Being built up over time — this isn't the full guide yet, just what we've covered so far.

## Pre-season checklist (so far)

- **Check the outside player data is ready before syncing.** We pull player info (names, positions, which club they're at) from the Fantasy Premier League website. Every summer, after promotion and relegation, it takes them a little while to update their team list. If a club is missing after you click "Import Players" on `/admin/sync`, it's usually because of this — see the To-do list (`docs/TODO.md`) for the current example (Coventry, Hull, Ipswich).
- **Mark relegated clubs as "inactive" and promoted clubs as "active"** on the Teams admin page, so players can't accidentally pick a team that's no longer in the competition.

More sections (deadlines, fixtures, quartiles, dress rehearsal) will get added here as we work through the rest of the pre-season setup.
